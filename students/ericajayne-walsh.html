Biography

Dangerously curious dreamer. Trying to make a dent in the universe. Always leaning. Constant change is my normal. Vegan. Minimalist. Digital Nomad. Non-conformist. Addicted to getting to know people and find out what drives them.

--Manifesto--

I believe in building meaningful connections, having real heartfelt conversations and trying to help the people around me have faith in themselves and go after their most audacious dreams. I believe in the power of networks and how we as a collective can help each other move humanity forward. My audacious dream is to help every person on the planet to feel like they matter and have found their place in the universe. I believe that the way to do this is to help people build real, genuine connection with the people they interact with everyday.
